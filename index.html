About the Host

Setsumi No Kami (pronounced set-soo-me) serves as the ethereal voice guiding you through the veils of Tsukihana Reika. With a gaze that pierces the mist and words that echo like distant chimes, Setsumi delivers the broadcasts with unwavering poise—reminding us all to stay vigilant amid the shifting shadows.

Daily transmissions (weather and cosmic anomalies permitting) bring you the latest from our ever-watchful moon, the river's capricious moods, and the subtle rearrangements of reality itself.
